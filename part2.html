Once finding this dataset I was curious as to which states had the largest increases in Alzheimer's related deaths over time. To answer this question, I first separated the data by cause of death and then by state. Then using linear regression on each 50 subsets of data, I was able to find the average year to year growth rate of Alzheimer's related deaths for each state. I then calculated the first, second, and third quartiles for use on my map.
